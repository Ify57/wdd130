Trip

Gardiner, Owerri

Embark on an adrenaline-pumping white water rafting expedition down the roaring rapids of the Otamiri River. Feel the rush as your expert guide navigates through Class III and IV rapids, challenging your teamwork and courage. With breathtaking scenery of steep canyon walls and glimpses of wildlife, this trip promises an unforgettable adventure for thrill-seekers and nature enthusiasts alike.

Taos, New Mexico

Join us for a family-friendly white water rafting adventure on the scenic New River. Glide through mild Class I and II rapids, perfect for beginners and children. Our knowledgeable guides will ensure safety while sharing stories of local history and wildlife. Enjoy the peaceful stretches between rapids, where you can swim, spot eagles soaring overhead, and soak in the beauty of untouched wilderness. This trip is ideal for creating lasting memories with loved ones in a relaxed, natural setting.

Canon City, Colorado

Experience the ultimate white water rafting expedition on a multi-day journey through the remote and pristine Salmon River. Immerse yourself in the untouched beauty of Owerri's wilderness as you navigate through challenging Class IV rapids and serene stretches of the river. Camp under the stars each night, sharing stories around the campfire and enjoying gourmet meals prepared by our skilled guides. This trip offers a unique blend of adventure, exploration, and camaraderie, making it perfect for adventurers seeking a true wilderness experience.
